FeatherReader

read, quietly

No signup. No password. No account here at all. Your atproto identity is your account — you'll approve access on your own server (Bluesky, or a PDS you host), and FeatherReader never sees your password. Your subscriptions, folders, stars, and read-state are stored as open-standard records in your PDS, portable to any reader that speaks community.lexicon.rss.*.

Open source · AGPL-3.0 · self-hostable
