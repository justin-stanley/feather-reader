About

FeatherReader is a calm, quiet reader — and a public experiment.

A typography-first RSS reader for people who left algorithmic feeds on purpose. Read, quietly.

This is an experiment

FeatherReader is run as a public experiment with no service-level agreement and no uptime guarantee. It may change, break, slow down, or be paused at any time, without notice. Please don't rely on it as your only reader — treat it as something to try, not something to depend on.

Your data lives in your PDS

There is no account here. You sign in with your own atproto identity, and your subscriptions, folders, stars, and read-state are written as open-standard community.lexicon.rss.* records in your own PDS — not on this server. Because your data is yours and portable, you can walk away at any time and take it with you. OPML export is always available, because leaving must be as easy as arriving.

Open source & self-hostable

FeatherReader is free software under the AGPL-3.0. You can read the code, file issues, or run your own copy — a single static binary plus a small OAuth sidecar. If you'd rather not depend on this instance, self-hosting is a first-class path.

Support the experiment

If FeatherReader is useful to you and you'd like to help keep it running, you can leave a tip ♥. It's a solo project; there's no obligation, and the reader stays free either way.
